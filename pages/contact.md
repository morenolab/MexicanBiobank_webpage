  <h2 id="contact-heading">Contact Us</h2>
  
  <p>The Mexican Biobank (MXB) welcomes inquiries regarding genomic research, institutional collaborations, and public outreach.</p>

  ### Research & Partnerships
  <p>For general questions, partnership proposals, or media inquiries, please reach out to our team at: 
  <strong><a href="mailto:morenolab.contact@gmail.com">morenolab.contact@gmail.com</a></strong></p>

  ### Data Access
  <p>To maintain genomic security and privacy, researchers seeking access to MXB genetic data must submit a formal request through the 
  <strong><a href="https://ega-archive.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">European Genome-Phenome Archive (EGA)</a></strong>.</p>

  <hr />

  <p>We look forward to fostering collaborations that advance genomic science in Mexico and globally.</p>
